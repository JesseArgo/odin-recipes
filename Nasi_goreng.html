Nasi goreng

Nasi goreng is Indonesia's national dish consisting of a rice with a very special preparation that can only be described as a delicious blessing

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 medium white onion
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 1 garlic glove
  • 1 cup of pre-cooked rice
  • 1/2 tsp shrimp paste
  • Sesame oil

Prep

  1. Scramble one egg and fry it up in a low-medium heat wok
  2. Chop in dices onion, red bell peppers and garlic, then add to the wok
  3. Mix it all in the wok and then add 1 cup of rice an keep mixing
  4. Now that everything is well mixed, create a hole in the center of the wok and add 1/2 tsp of shrimp paste
  5. Now add some kecap and salt and mix again
    • Recommendation: add some kecap mix and then try it, if you want more add just don't add too much at one time or it can get a strong and no so good flavor
  6. And a small touch of sesame oil
  7. Now serve and top with a crispy fried egg and some sliced green onion
  8. Time to enjoy
